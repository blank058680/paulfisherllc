About the Firm

A Legacy of Integrity, Advocacy & Results

Founded in 2004, Paul Fischer LLC has grown into one of Berlin's most respected full-service law firms. We combine the resources of a large practice with the personal attention of a boutique every client works directly with a senior lawyer who knows their matter inside out.

Our mission is simple: to protect what matters most to you. We pursue that mission with rigour, candour, and an unwavering commitment to the people and businesses we serve.
