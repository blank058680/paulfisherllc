Our Story

Two Decades of Principled Advocacy

Paul Fischer LLC was founded in 2004 by Paul Fischer and Eleanor Wray on a simple conviction: that world-class legal counsel should be rigorous, transparent, and genuinely personal. Two decades later, that conviction still guides every matter we take on.

Today we are a full-service firm of more than 35 solicitors serving individuals, families, and businesses across Berlin and Germany yet we have never lost the boutique instinct to treat every client as our most important.
